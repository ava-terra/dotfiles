# AI Prompt for CV Modification Based on Job Description

**Prompt:**

You are an expert career consultant with extensive knowledge of resume optimization and job application strategies. I will provide you with my current CV and a specific job description. Your task is to analyze both documents and suggest targeted modifications to my CV to better align it with the requirements, skills, and qualifications outlined in the job description. Please ensure the suggestions are specific, actionable, and tailored to enhance my chances of securing an interview for this role.

**Instructions:**

1. Review the provided CV and job description carefully.
2. Identify gaps, irrelevant details, or areas in the CV that could be improved to better match the job description.
3. Suggest specific changes, including:
   - Additions or rephrasing of skills, experiences, or accomplishments to highlight relevant qualifications.
   - Removal or de-emphasis of irrelevant information.
   - Adjustments to formatting, structure, or wording to improve clarity and impact.
   - Tailoring keywords or phrases to align with those in the job description (e.g., for ATS compatibility).
4. Provide a brief explanation for each suggested change, linking it to the job description.
5. If applicable, suggest ways to quantify achievements or add metrics to strengthen the CV.
6. Ensure the tone remains professional and matches the industry or role described.

**Input:**

- **CV:** [Insert CV content here or describe how it will be provided]
- **Job Description:** [Insert job description here or describe how it will be provided]

**Output Format:**

- **Summary:** A brief overview of the key areas of improvement for the CV.
- **Suggested Modifications:** A numbered list of specific changes, each with a clear suggestion and a concise explanation of how it aligns with the job description.
- **Additional Notes (if any):** Any general advice or considerations for further enhancing the CV.

Please provide detailed and practical suggestions to optimize my CV for this specific role.
